Risk Management

Capital Preservation First.

Seven layers of protection ensure that no single trade, event, or system failure can compromise your capital.

Portfolio Heat Control

Total portfolio risk capped at 6% of capital at all times. Automatically prevents new positions when limit is reached.

Correlation Filtering

Detects correlated positions and applies sizing penalties. Prevents concentration risk across similar instruments.

Drift Auto-Halt

If the drift detector scores above 0.7, all trading halts automatically. No manual intervention required.

News Event Shield

Reduces confidence by up to 70% during high-impact economic events. Configurable buffer windows before and after.

Walk-Forward Validation

Every ML model is validated on unseen future data. No training-testing overlap. No backtest illusions.

Maximum Drawdown Limit

Hard stop at 8% portfolio drawdown. System pauses and requires manual review before resuming operations.

Session-Aware Sizing

Position sizes adjust based on trading session. Asian session: reduced size. London-NY overlap: full allocation.

Execution Quality Gate

Monitors slippage, fill quality, and latency per trade. Alerts and reduces sizing if execution deteriorates.
